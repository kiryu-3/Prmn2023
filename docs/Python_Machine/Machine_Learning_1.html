重回帰分析の流れ¶



モデルを決める¶

$x_{ij}$
$j$$i$

$\hat{y}_i$

$$ \hat{y}_i = \theta_1x_{i1} + \theta_2x_{i2} + + \theta_nx_{in} $$

$\theta$


$b$

$$ \hat{y}_i = \theta_1x_{i1} + \theta_2x_{i2} + + \theta_nx_{in} + b $$

$b = \theta_0x_0 = \theta_01$

$$ \hat{y}_i = \theta_1x_{i1} + \theta_2x_{i2} + + \theta_nx_{in} + \theta_0x_{i0} $$

$$ \begin{bmatrix} \hat{y}_1 \\ \hat{y}_2 \\ \vdots \\ \hat{y}_{m-1} \\ \hat{y}_m \\ \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} x_{10} & x_{11} & \cdots & x_{1n-1} & x_{1n} \\ x_{20} & x_{21} & \cdots & x_{2n-1} & x_{2n} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \cdots & \vdots & \vdots \\ x_{m-10} & x_{m-11} & \cdots & x_{m-1n-1} & x_{m-1n} \\ x_{m0} & x_{m1} & \cdots & x_{mn-1} & x_{mn} \\ \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \theta_0 \\ \theta_1 \\ \vdots \\ \vdots \\ \theta_{n-1} \\ \theta_n \\ \end{bmatrix} $$

$\mathbf{\hat{y}},\mathbf{X},\theta$

$$ \mathbf{\hat{y}}=\mathbf{X}\mathbf{\theta} $$

損失関数を決める¶

$$ \sum^{n}_{i=1}{e_i^2}= \sum^{n}_{i=1}({y_i-\hat{y}_i)^2} $$

損失関数を「最小化」する¶

$$ \theta=(\mathbf{X}^T\mathbf{X})^{-1}\mathbf{X}^T\mathbf{y} $$

決定係数の定義¶




$$ \sum (y_i-\bar{y})^2 = \sum(\hat{y}_i-\bar{y})^2 + \sum(y_i-\hat{y}_i)^2 $$


$$ R^2 = \frac{\sum(\hat{y}_i-\bar{y})^2}{\sum (y_i-\bar{y})^2} = 1-\frac{\sum(y_i-\hat{y}_i)^2}{\sum (y_i-\bar{y})^2} $$
